niutero connector

Capture citations into your local niutero library. Start the connector from a terminal, then paste its session token below. 

niutero-cli connector <vault>

Must match the connector's --port (default 23510).

Printed once when the connector starts. It is stored only on this machine and only ever sent to 127.0.0.1.

How to use

  • Open a paper's page (publisher, arXiv, ACL, PubMed, Google Scholar…).
  • Click the niutero toolbar button, or press Alt+Shift+S, or right-click → “Capture citation to niutero”.
  • Pages with a DOI resolve to canonical BibTeX on the server; others are built from the page's citation meta tags.
